🛠️ Quick Troubleshooting Tips

Common fixes for everyday issues. If something feels off, stop and verify before proceeding.

My computer is suddenly slow or hot
  • Close tabs/apps you don’t need and restart the device.
  • Run a full malware scan with your security software.
  • Check Task Manager/Activity Monitor for unknown apps using high CPU.
  • Update OS and apps; patches often include performance and security fixes.
I clicked a suspicious link
  • Disconnect from Wi‑Fi if the page looked malicious; don’t enter any passwords.
  • Run a malware scan and clear your browser cache/history.
  • Change passwords for affected accounts and enable 2‑factor authentication.
  • Watch for unexpected password reset emails or new login alerts.
My account might be compromised
  • Immediately reset the password to a strong, unique one.
  • Review account activity and revoke unknown sessions/devices.
  • Turn on multi‑factor authentication (prefer an authenticator app over SMS).
  • Update recovery email/phone and remove outdated ones.
Wi‑Fi is unreliable or unknown devices are connected
  • Reboot the modem/router; update firmware from the vendor site.
  • Change the Wi‑Fi password and admin password; disable WPS.
  • Use WPA2/WPA3, and split smart‑home devices onto a guest network.
  • Rename SSID to something generic; avoid personal info in the name.

⚠️ Things to Watch Out For

Recognize common scams and risky patterns. When in doubt, pause and verify via official channels.

Phishing Emails & Texts

“Your account is locked” or “Unusual sign‑in detected.”

Red flag Urgent tone + links to sign in.

Do Go to the site directly; don’t use message links.

MFA Fatigue Attacks

Repeated push notifications asking you to approve a login.

Red flag Approvals you didn’t initiate.

Do Deny and change password; switch to code‑based MFA.

Tech Support Scams

Pop‑ups “from Microsoft/Apple” asking you to call a number.

Red flag Requests for remote access or gift cards.

Do Close the tab; contact support via the official site.

Fake Invoices & Job Offers

Attachments requiring payment or “urgent onboarding” forms.

Check Sender domain and request via a known channel.

Do Verify with the company’s website or HR before acting.

QR Code & Link Shorteners

Codes posted in public or shortened links in messages.

Red flag Unexpected payment or login prompts.

Do Preview destination; use your camera’s link preview feature.

Crypto & Investment Promises

Guaranteed returns, celebrity endorsements, or urgent timelines.

Red flag Pressure to move money quickly to new accounts.

Do Stop and consult a trusted person or advisor.
